## Intro

I take a small amount of paid work alongside my MS: tutoring in quantum
machine learning, backend consulting on .NET/Azure systems, and research
office hours. Everything below is delivered by me personally — no agency, no
handoff. Booking and payment run through external services; if a link below
is still an email, the service is available but the booking page isn't up
yet.

## Quantum ML tutoring

**Pitch:** One-on-one sessions on quantum computing and quantum machine
learning — from qubit foundations to variational classifiers and
quanvolutional networks, the exact material of my [B.Sc.
thesis](/research/quantum-machine-learning-thesis) and the [interactive
curriculum](/learn) on this site. Ideal for students starting a QML thesis
or engineers who want a working mental model, not just formulas.

**Price:** rates on request — student-friendly

**CTA:** [email to book a session](mailto:abuammarsami@gmail.com?subject=QML%20tutoring)

## Backend consulting — .NET / Azure

**Pitch:** Short consulting blocks on production backend problems:
API design, payment integrations, Azure services, background processing,
and performance work — the systems I build in my day job. Best used as a
focused review or unblocking session on a concrete problem, with written
notes afterwards.

**Price:** rates on request

**CTA:** [email to discuss scope](mailto:abuammarsami@gmail.com?subject=Backend%20consulting)

## Research office hours

**Pitch:** A low-key slot for students and early researchers: reading-group
style discussion of a QML/ML paper, feedback on a thesis draft or research
plan, or an honest "is this direction viable?" conversation. The same
honesty contract as the rest of this site applies — if I don't know, I say
so.

**Price:** free for students, within limits

**CTA:** [email to request a slot](mailto:abuammarsami@gmail.com?subject=Office%20hours)
